FAQ

Common questions

Why is the response taking so long?

When JO AI is waking up or temporarily unavailable, responses may take longer or may not work temporarily. Please wait and try again, or contact @grpbuyer3.

How do I copy a response quickly?

Use the Copy last button at the top of the output area, or use the copy button on a response bubble.

Why is my image not uploading in JO AI Vision?

Make sure the file is a common image type such as PNG, JPG, JPEG, WEBP, GIF, or BMP and is not too large.

Why is there no conversation on a page yet?

The history area fills after you send your first request. Use Clear history if you want a fresh page.
